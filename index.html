The Present
Moment

We live in a world defined by paradox: the more connected we are, the more divided we become; the more productive, the less fulfilled; technologically empowered, but powerless to technology.

Our current moment is one in which we are all searching for ways to feel less rushed, anxious, lonely.

We’re ready for more.

We’re increasingly understanding mindfulness as part of a solution to our modern day maladies. But the popular notion of mindful practice remains narrow.

Millions of people today are still sitting alone in their living rooms, peeking at the clock, wondering if it’s “working” yet. But as mindfulness continues to grow in popularity, we’re seeking new ways to deepen, expand, and explore the practice.

Open broadens the world of modern mindfulness, drawing from practices old and new, designing varied, reliable ways in to the present moment.

Our approach grounds the experience in your senses, using music, sound, breath, and movement to interrupt the chatter in our minds and anchor in the here and now.

We believe that mindfulness is more. More than a form of stress-relief — it can be a tool for personal growth, deeper understanding, vibrancy. More than a way to disconnect — it can be a community, a way to feel a part of something bigger. More than simply silence — it can be immersive, beautiful, moving.

There is great power in the present, and we do everything in our power to bring you there.
